Fight Club

I am Jack's cult movie phenomenon. On top of the impressing visuals of the then newcomer David Fincher the film Fight Club has become a movie that is worshiped among college students everywhere. Could it be the idea of using a fight as a form of therapy that appeals the most? Or is it the undertone of anti-consumerism that ripples throughout the film as a whole? We may never know but one thing is for sure, Fight Club is sticking around for a while.
