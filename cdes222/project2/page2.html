Se7en

What's in the box?!?! This gripping crime thriller staring Brad Pitt and Morgan "That Voice" Freeman pins the two detectives against a mysterious man that's murdering according to the seven deadly sins. Despite it's great visuals and preformances from both Freeman and Pitt, this movie is not for the faint of heart. However, if you are able to stomach the violence and gore you may be able to find out what is truly in that box...
