LAZARETH WAZUMA V8F


Ok, stop us if you’ve heard this one: What do you get when you cross a Ferrari, BMW, and a quad? Give up yet? You get the Lazareth Wazuma V8F Matte Edition. And believe us when we say, the Wazuma is no f’ing joke. Strap on your helmet and buckle up as you are basically sitting on a 250hp Ferrari 3.0L V-8 connected to a six speed BMW M3 gearbox and a handful of steam roller sized tires. While we can’t be certain what the Wazuma is even the slightest bit practical for, we do know that for those that can afford the $250,000 sticker price, you will likely own the fastest quad on the planet.
